Ingredients:
 - 1 pound brussels sprouts
 - 3 to 4 tablespoons olive oil
 salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
 1. Trim ends of brussles sprouts, cut in half lengthwise throught the stem end
 2. Heat the oil in a large, heavy skillet over medium-high heat
 3. Place cut side of brussles sprouts down on the oil and reduce heat to medium
 4. Sear until browned, about three minutes
 5. Turn brussels sprouts over and cook the other side another three to five minutes
 6. Remove from heat and season with salt and pepper

Notes:
 Recipe by Martha Rose Shulman of the New York Times
